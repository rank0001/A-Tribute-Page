Here is the life of Paul Walker in short:

  • 1973 - Born in Glendale,California
  • 1985 - Began working on television shows
  • 1986 - Film career began with the movie Monster in the closet
  • 1991 - Graduated from Sun Valley's Village Christian School
  • 2001 - Did the blockbuster film the fast and the furious with Vin Diesel
  • 2003 - First sequel of the fast and the furious 2 fast 2 furious
  • 2011 - Became the face of the brand for Davidoff Cool Water for Men
  • 2013 - Died in a fatal car crash
